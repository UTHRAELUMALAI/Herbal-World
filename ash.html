Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera)

Ashwagandha, known as **Indian Ginseng**, is a powerful adaptogenic herb in Ayurveda. It helps the body manage stress, boosts stamina, enhances memory, and promotes restful sleep. It balances **Vata and Kapha** doshas, rejuvenating both mind and body.
