1. Tulsi Tea for Immunity

Boil 5-7 fresh tulsi leaves in a cup of water for 5 minutes. Add a slice of ginger and a few drops of honey. Drink warm daily on an empty stomach to boost your immune system.

2. Tulsi Juice for Skin Health

Grind fresh tulsi leaves into a paste and extract the juice. Mix with turmeric and apply on acne or blemishes for clearer skin. Tulsi purifies the blood and reduces inflammation.

3. Tulsi Steam Inhalation

Boil tulsi leaves in water and inhale the steam. This clears nasal passages, relieves sinus congestion, and acts as a natural decongestant.

4. Tulsi Oil for Stress Relief

Mix tulsi essential oil with coconut oil and massage it onto temples and the back of the neck. It calms the mind, relieves headaches, and reduces anxiety.

5. Tulsi-Cinnamon Water for Blood Sugar

Soak tulsi leaves and a cinnamon stick in water overnight. Drink this first thing in the morning to help stabilize blood sugar and improve metabolism.

6. Tulsi Kadha (Decoction) for Cold and Cough

Boil tulsi, ginger, black pepper, clove, and a pinch of turmeric in 2 cups of water until it reduces to half. Strain and drink warm with honey for relief from sore throat and chest congestion.
